Tissue Engineering and Organ Regeneration

Goal of the Subject

Engineer treatments for medical problems

  1. Introduce tools: biomaterials, cells, and molecular regulators
  2. Identify cell and molecular targets for treatment

Why It’s Urgent

  1. People around the world are living longer, but pain and disability have substantially degraded the quality of their lives and many of them cannot access and/or tolerate surgery
  2. There are not enough surgeons to meet the needs of patients, globally.
    • Need to provide nonsurgical (injectable) treatments at an earlier stage in the disease process
    • Need to include in medical device design process delivery by nonsurgical personnel

Key Concepts

  • Tissue engineering: in vitro tissue production
  • Organ regeneration: in vivo healing

    • And some additional definitions

  • Four main cell types: connective, epithelial, muscle, nerve
  • Tissue: A group of cells that have similar make-up and behaviour
  • Organ: A structure comprising two or more tissues
  • Engineering: Making a product using existing knowledge
  • Science: Process of acquiring new knowledge
  • Technology: Means of producing the product
  • Dictionary: Medical Dictionary
  • The human body:A collection of organs and tissues connected by "connective tissue" and vascular and lympatic systems, and bound to the musculoskeletal system
